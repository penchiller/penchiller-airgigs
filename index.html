Absolutely. That's exactly what the creative brief is for. I write across Pop, R&B, Hip-Hop, Gospel, Country, EDM, Soul, Afrobeats, Folk, and more. Share references, moods, or a concept — I'll make it fit.

Every package includes revisions — 1 with The Seed, 3 with The Anthem, and unlimited with The Legacy. If something doesn't feel right, just explain what's off and I'll rework it until you love it.

Of course. Send me the file and I'll write directly to the track — matching the energy, tempo, and emotional pocket of your production.
